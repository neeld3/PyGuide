Lesson 1: Printing in Python

What does “print” mean in Python?

In Python, print means “show something on the screen.” It's like asking the computer to say something, but using text.

print("Hello!")

When run, it will show:

Hello!

Why do we use printing?

Printing helps us check our work, make the computer talk, and see what our program is doing.
